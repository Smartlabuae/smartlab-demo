01 — WORKSPACES

Lab Benches & Worktops

Reconfigurable workbenches built on sturdy epoxy-coated frames, with worktops in epoxy resin, ceramic or stainless steel. Cabinets reposition easily so layouts adapt as your lab evolves — ideal for QC, R&D and teaching labs alike.

  • Island, wall and peninsula configurations
  • Chemical-resistant, non-porous worktops
  • Integrated services: power, gas, water
02 — EXTRACTION

Fume Hoods

Ducted and ductless fume cupboards designed for safe, efficient containment of fumes and vapours. Engineered airflow, durable liners and clear sash visibility keep operators protected during sensitive procedures.

  • Bench-top and walk-in models
  • Corrosion-resistant interior liners
  • Energy-efficient airflow control
03 — STORAGE

Safety Storage Cabinets

Purpose-built cabinets for the safe storage of chemicals, acids, bases and flammable materials — keeping your facility organised and compliant with safety regulations.

  • Flammable & corrosive storage options
  • Ventilation-ready designs
  • Clear labelling and secure closures
04 — FITTINGS

Laboratory Fittings

A full range of lab fittings and accessories: epoxy and stainless sinks, gooseneck taps, gas and water service fixtures, emergency eye-wash and safety showers — everything that completes a working lab.

  • Drip cups, sinks and waste systems
  • Gas, air, vacuum & water taps
  • Eye-wash and emergency shower units

Lab Seating & Stools

Ergonomic laboratory chairs and stools with easy-clean, chemical-resistant upholstery and adjustable height — supporting comfort and posture through long working hours.
